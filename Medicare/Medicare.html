From The Director’s Desk

Our foundation stands on the philosophy of putting patients first in everything we do and this can be seen at Medicare
Hospital at all levels. We have been accredited by NABH/NABL. We care utmost for our patients by providing high quality
healthcare services; not only do we say it but our patients also affirm the same.

As we enter our 33rd year, I want to reaffirm this commitment – Medicare Hospital will always bring you the best healthcare
at an affordable price. With over 100 beds & a team of 35+ doctors and our 24/7 service, we bring you the best and
comprehensive health care whenever you need it. We are equipped to provide complete support to patients along with their families.

Our laboratories have been the foundation of our success. We have started a free home care service for those who could not
afford to come to the hospital. We have taken several initiatives to provide the best healthcare to all the people who come to
us and we are striving for better each and every day.

Final outcome of treatment of any disease depends on accurate diagnosis and workup.We are proud to say that we have
unparalleled quality conscious dedicated team for investigation required to make diagnosis such as clinical pathology,
biochemistry, microbiology, histopathology, ultrasonography, radiology and urodynamics studies…!

About Medicare Hospital
Medicare Hospital & Research Center was established in 1990 to provide affordable medical care in a patient-friendly
atmosphere and in the spirit of compassion, previously beyond the reach of the common man. Its aim was to provide high-quality
medical services at an affordable cost.

This multi-specialty 100 bedded hospital is renowned for its medical expertise, excellent nursing care and quality diagnostics.
Specialists on the hospital panel include some of the most eminent names in the Indian Medical Profession.
