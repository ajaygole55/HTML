adidas has a wide range of high-quality slides and flip flops that are designed for multiple different uses by the water.
Dedicated adidas flip flops and slide sandals are perfect for swimming, showering and casual strolls at the pool or beach.
Practical, stylish and highly comfortable, the Adilette line of sandals and flip flops has long been a big hit with watersports fans.
The Adilette Comfort Slides, for example, feature a Cloudfoam+ footbed that offers extraordinary comfort and energy
for your feet to recharge you after a tiring swim. The injected EVA outsole guarantees comfort with its lightweight build,
while the contoured footbed and single-bandage moulded EVA upper ensure a perfect fit for stability as you walk.
Cloudfoam slides are also available with synthetic material uppers. Aside from these adult models of adidas slides,
you can find equally great versions for your kids. Infants’ swim sandals come in different fun colour options and feature easily
adjustable straps that allow for years of use as they accommodate growing feet. The contoured footbed is extra-comfy and dries quickly, making them the ideal footwear for trips to the pool or the beach.
The EVA upper is lightweight, which guarantees that feet won’t get strained, while an adidas logo on the side provides easily identifiable branding that will look good on feet of any age.
